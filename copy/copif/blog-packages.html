How it works...

Choose your package

Tell us how many posts you would like and what subjects you would like our blog writers to cover. You can change these details as often as you like to ensure you are completely happy with the posts you receive.

I have ordered a blog package, what happens next?

Once you have signed up for a package, you will be given access to an online dashboard where you can manage your account.

How do you know what to write about?

We will base your content around the keywords you provide. You can change these at any time, or give further instruction on the type of content you would like to receive.

Can I change my package?

Absolutely. You can change either the number of posts you receive, or the subject of your posts at any time through our easy to use dashboard.

What if I'm not happy with my blog posts?

If you’re not happy with the posts your receive, you can change your instructions to the writers to ensure that the content is more to your liking. If you are still not satisfied, you can cancel your package at any time.

Can you publish my posts for me?

For those using WordPress you can install the Copify Plugin which will enable you to publish posts instantly through your dashboard. We'll even give you an image as well!

Can I review my copy before publishing?

Sure. You can review your copy through our online dashboard.
